About the Project

What is Fairness Auditing?

Understanding why unbiased AI matters in today's world.

⚠️

The Problem

AI systems used in hiring, lending, and healthcare can perpetuate biases from training data — leading to discriminatory outcomes against protected groups.

🔍

Our Approach

We build an end-to-end auditing pipeline that automatically detects bias, computes fairness metrics, mitigates disparities, and explains model decisions.

📊

The Result

A transparent fairness dashboard with statistical metrics, SHAP feature importance, group-level analysis, and downloadable audit reports.
